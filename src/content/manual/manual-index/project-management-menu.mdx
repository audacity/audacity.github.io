# Project Toolbar

_This article describes the Project Toolbar at the top of the window — its navigation tabs, quick action buttons, workspace selector, and the Audio Setup menu._

The Project Toolbar sits at the top of the window.

## Navigation tabs

- **Home** — view your local projects.
- **Project** — open the editor. If you don't have a project open yet, Audacity creates a new one for you.
- **Export** — switch to an export-focused layout, where the Transport Toolbar shows Play, Stop, Loop, Share, Export audio, and Export loop region.

Use the arrow keys to move between these tabs.

## Quick actions

- **Mixer** — open the Mixer panel.
- **Audio setup** — open the Audio Setup menu (see below).
- **Share audio** — open the Share dialog to upload or share your project.
- **Get effects** — browse the Effects Marketplace for additional effects.

## Workspace and history

- **Workspace** — choose between Music, Classic, Modern, and Spectral editing layouts.
- **Undo** / **Redo**.

## Audio Setup menu

Opened from the **Audio setup** button:

- **Playback device** — choose which output device audio plays through.
- **Recording device**, **Recording channels**, **Rescan audio devices**, **Audio settings**, and **Host** — _not available in this version_.
