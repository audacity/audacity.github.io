- **[How keyboard navigation works](/manual/accessibility/navigation-model)** — the tab-group model, roving focus rules, and the global tab order
- **[Tracks, clips and labels](/manual/accessibility/track-view)** — navigating and editing in the track area, including clip editing, playhead and time-selection shortcuts
- **[Toolbars and panels](/manual/accessibility/toolbars-and-panels)** — the menu bar, toolbars, effects panel, timeline ruler and application-wide shortcuts
- **[Modals and menus](/manual/accessibility/modals-and-menus)** — the export and preferences dialogs and context menus

Each page ends with implementation notes covering the details that are easy to get wrong.
